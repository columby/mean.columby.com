About Columby

Columby helps you create an online page per dataset with all relevant information in one clear overview which you can use as a unique source in all your communications. We process your data and make them available for downloading, embedded previews or distribution via our API.

  • Create dataset pages
  • Upload or sync your data
  • Add references
  • Distribute your data with our API
  • Create collections
  • Manage your account with colleagues
  • Register for free

Publish your data in a beautiful way.

You carefully gather your data. We help you to present it in it's best way. Attach beautiful pictures, describe your dataset and add some references.

Embed your data with ease.

Distributing your data has become easy. Use our API to integrate your data in your apps or embed datasets on your website.
